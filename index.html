Wondering why you are longing, feeling melancholic, or nostalgic?

Do you think that the Covid-19 pandemic trigger a widespread nostalgia?

According to an article pubished on National Geographic on 21 July 2020, more than half of consumers reported finding comfort in television show, music and films that they have consumed in their youth.


In this experiment, I will explore the sentiment of nostalgia, and will try to identify and manipulate images and create animated GIFs as a way to represent fond memories.

In my native language, Portuguese, the word 'saudade' is widely used to express how nostalgic we feel about anything, a person, an object or an animal.
