HomeAway, find your perfect stay.

On this part of the assignment, I will explore the website that I have developed for the Web Project side of the module.

The website is called HomeAway and it is loosely inspired by AirBnB.

For this website, I have created the logo, specified the the font-family and the colour palette which I intended to use.

2) Popular destination on the Homepage

On the homepage there are two featured destinations, Rio de Janeiro and Lisbon, when the viewer hover over the images they expand giving further information about them.



3) Brand colours

For the visual design of the website, I have chosen the following colours: Shilo (RGB 230 172 172), Hollywood Cerise (RGB 236,0,140), Seal Brown (RGB 65,27,27) and Snow (RGB 255,250,250).


4) Brand logo

For the website logo, I have created it on Adobe Photoshop using the font-family: Ubuntu, which I have downloaded from Google Fonts.

4) Website buttons

For the website buttons, I have created it on Adobe InDesign a mock-up og how it would change its colours once the website user hover over it. Changing its colour fill from Snow (RGB 255,250,250) to Shilo (RGB 230 172 172).
